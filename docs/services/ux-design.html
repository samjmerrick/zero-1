UX Design

What is UX design?

Our UX design team craft beautiful and intuitive experiences that solve real user needs.

Our Competencies

Wireframing

We start by creating the structural flow of a product as simply as possible. We consider the goals, risks and challenges a user might face at each step.

This gives us the flexibility to take a step back before committing to a set style. We research, validate and make changes to the flow as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Contact us for help with Wireframing.

Prototyping

We create end-to-end experiences using low-code or no-code solutions.

This allows us to check the effectiveness of the product in a mock environment, before commmitting to costly development.

Our prototyping stack

  • Figma
  • Adobe XD
  • Bubble.io
  • WebFlow
  • ProtoPie

Contact us to help with Prototyping.

User Research

We conduct user research at every opportunity, using leading techniques that avoid bias and uncover truth.

This ensures that the experiences we create solve real user needs. As a result, our products are useful, usable and desirable.

Our research methods

  • Surveys
  • Depth interviews
  • Focus groups
  • Guerilla research

Contact us for help with User Research.
